Industry-Specific Modules

Connect hosts a range of industry solutions, the first modules available today are:

Introducing

GeoSLAM Connect

Automatic SLAM processing is here – plug in your ZEB and Connect will see the new data and run a workflow script of your choice, creating a pointcloud as you want it.

Choose where data is stored

If you have a network folder, or centralized file storage, you can choose where your raw or processed data and output reports will be stored. Each scan will be automatically uploaded, making data sharing and collaboration easier than ever.

Script based workflows

Tell Connect how you want your data processed via workflow scripts, and it’ll process each data set for you, automatically. Choose from default workflows, or customize your own in the user interface.

3D viewer

Visualize your automatically processed scan in the integrated viewer, with options for colorisation and filtering.

Phone app

Integrate photos, notes, voice recordings and custom asset tags to your point cloud, for adding context to your scans.
